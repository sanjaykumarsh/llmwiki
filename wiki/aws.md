# AWS

**Summary**: Amazon Web Services (AWS) is a cloud computing platform used for enterprise solution architecture.

---

## Explanation
AWS is recognized as a standard for solution architecture in modern enterprise environments (source: Kumar_Sanjay_Res_Final v4.2_architect_citi.pdf). Professionals may hold associate-level certifications to validate their expertise in designing and deploying scalable infrastructure (source: Kumar_Sanjay_Res_Final v4.2_architect_citi.pdf).
