# Citi

**Summary**: A multinational financial services corporation that utilizes enterprise-level architecture for its finance and compliance portfolios.

---

## Explanation
Citi provides a complex environment for IT initiatives, specifically involving cloud implementations, Big Data solutions, and enterprise container services (source: Kumar_Sanjay_Res_Final v4.2_architect_citi.pdf). Technical architects at Citi are responsible for enforcing data standards, security architecture reviews, and managing technology roadmaps (source: Kumar_Sanjay_Res_Final v4.2_architect_citi.pdf).
